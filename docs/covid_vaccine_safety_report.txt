COVID-19 Vaccine Safety Surveillance Report
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
Last Updated: October 2023

Key Findings:
- mRNA vaccines show excellent safety profile
- Rare side effects: Myocarditis (2-5 cases per 100,000 doses)
- No long-term safety concerns identified

Monitoring Systems:
- VAERS (Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System)
- V-Safe active surveillance
- CDC Vaccine Safety Datalink

Recommendations:
- Benefits outweigh risks for all approved age groups
- Booster doses recommended for immunocompromised
